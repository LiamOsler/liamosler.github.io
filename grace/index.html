Death Doula

Looking for peace and dignity throughout your terminal illness and/or end of life journey?

I am a palliative care nurse and death doula and am available to provide compassionate support for you and your loved ones throughout your unique journey.

Elegant Grace End of Life Doulas is a registered business with flexible rates to make end of life care affordable to you and your loved ones, according to your needs.

Services

Services include but are not limited to:

  • End of life care and support
  • Legacy work life review
  • Advanced care planning and goals of care
  • Guidance through your unique choices
  • Illness journey support
  • Death, funeral and vigil planning
  • Grief and bereavement support
  • Other services available, please contact for consultation
